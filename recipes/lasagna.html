Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 24oz marinara sauce
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzerella

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown the beef
  3. add in seasonings then cover to cook with the tomoato sauce
  4. bring water to a boil in a pot to cook the noodles
  5. Add salt to the water and drop in noodles
  6. Take noodles out and place them in cold water
  7. Get a 9X13 inch casserole dish
  8. Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom
  9. Add 3 noodles then 1 cup of mozzarella and ricotta cheese
  10. Repeat this process for remaining ingredients
  11. Cook for 45 minutes
