Chilaquiles Verdes

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Crema
  • Queso fresco
  • Eggs
  • Cilantro
  • Tomatillo
  • Onion
  • Serranos
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Cut tortillas into small squares
  2. Fry tortillas in oil until crispy
  3. Set tortillas on paper towel to drain excess oil
  4. In a blender add tomatillos, onion, serranos, garlic, and salt to taste. Blend until smooth
  5. In a saucepan add blended sauce and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Add fried tortillas to the sauce and stir to coat
  7. Cook for 5 more minutes
  8. In a separate pan cook eggs to your liking
  9. Plate chilaquiles and top with crema, queso fresco, cilantro, and eggs
